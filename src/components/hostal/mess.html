DO'S & DON'TS OF HOSTEL

General Rules

  1. Students must maintain dignity and discipline while staying in the hostel. They must obey the hostel warden/floor in-charges.
  2. Valuable items like gold should not be kept in the hostel. Students are responsible for their laptops and money. Management will not be responsible for any losses.
  3. Students must inform hostel authorities before filing police complaints for any losses.
  4. Students are strictly prohibited from possessing or using cigarettes, beer, alcohol, gutka, drugs, etc.
  5. Students must hand over room keys to security when leaving and collect them upon return.
  6. Students must switch off fans, lights, geysers, A/Cs, etc., before leaving their rooms.

Visitors & Timings

  1. Visitors are not allowed into the hostel. They can meet in the visitor's hall with prior permission from the warden.
  2. Only family members listed by parents are allowed to contact students.
  3. Visiting hours are up to 7:30 PM only.
  4. 1st-year students must return to hostel by 3:00 PM, others by 6:00 PM.
  5. Students using computer lab/library after specified times must submit permission slips to security.

Study Hours & Discipline

  1. Strict study hours from 7:30 PM to 10:30 PM must be maintained.
  2. Students must be in their allotted rooms during study hours.
  3. Complaints must be registered in the complaint register at the hostel office.
  4. Students must maintain regular college attendance.
  5. Students are not allowed to stay in hostel during college working hours without prior permission.

Room Maintenance & Restrictions

  1. Students must clean their rooms and dispose of waste in corridor bins.
  2. Fine of Rs.100/- for unclean rooms during inspection.
  3. Electrical appliances like water heaters are prohibited.
  4. Electronic gadgets like tape recorders, video games are not allowed.
  5. Laptops are permitted for academic purposes only.
  6. Generator facility is available from 6:00 PM to 11:00 PM for lighting and fans.

Mess Rules

  1. Hostel inmates must not remove any mess items (glasses, plates, etc.).
  2. First violation: Fine of Rs.100/-
  3. Second violation: Fine of Rs.300/-
  4. Third violation: Expulsion from hostel

Important Notes

  1. Ragging is strictly prohibited as per the Ragging Act 1997.
  2. Violation can result in imprisonment, fine up to Rs.10,000/-, and dismissal.
  3. Cell phones are strictly prohibited in the hostel.
  4. Unauthorized cell phone usage will result in seizure and Rs.500/- fine.
  5. TV viewing is allowed only between 5:00 PM to 7:30 PM on regular days.
  6. TV is completely stopped during examinations.
